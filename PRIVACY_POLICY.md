# Privacy Policy for LibreMeet

LibreMeet does not collect any personal data.

The application runs entirely on your local machine. Audio recordings, transcripts, meeting summaries, and all configuration data stay on your device. No data is sent to any server. No analytics telemetry is transmitted. There is no telemetry infrastructure in the codebase.

If you choose to use a cloud-based AI provider for summaries (OpenAI, Anthropic, Groq, etc.), the transcript text you submit is handled by that provider under their privacy policy. This is optional and configured per-meeting.

Questions or concerns: open an issue on GitHub.
